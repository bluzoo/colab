Covid Color Lab (CoLab)

The global pandemic has forever changed our expectations for public spaces and social gatherings. The Covid Color Lab responds to this decline of our communal experience.

As individual memories about the pandemic encounter, merge, and clash with each other in CoLab, we invite the visitor to explore unthought possibilities of connection between human being during a moment of crisis.
